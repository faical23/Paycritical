More detailed information about cookies and their operation can be found here.

What cookies do we use and why

Some of the cookies we use are required to allow browsing this Website as well as taking advantage of its features such as accessing secure areas and exclusive access content for registered users.


Our Website also uses functional cookies to record information about the options of our users allowing them to customize our Website to their needs; for example, to memorize the language of origin or region or that a user has completed the completion of an inquiry. The information recorded is anonymous and is intended only for the purpose indicated above.

We may use webanalytics services, directly or indirectly, to gauge the effectiveness of our content and the preferences of our users that allow us to contribute to optimizing the functioning of this Website. In addition, we use web beacons or tracking pixels to count the number of visitors and performance cookies to monitor how users individually access our website and how regularly. This information is only used for statistical purposes without identification of any particular user. However, for registered users who are connected to the Website we may use the combined use of this information with the data collected via webanalytics services and cookies to analyze how visitors use this Website in more detail.

Whenever you want detailed information about the cookies used on our website we invite you to contact us through the email form.
